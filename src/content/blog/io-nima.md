AyTi'da I/O degan tushuncha bor. Input va Output. Ya'ni kirish va chiqish.

Eng oddiy misol bilan:

Klaviaturada bosilgan tugma - input.

Ekranda paydo bo'lgan belgi - output.

Texnik tomondan I/O shu.

O'ylashimcha, hayotimiz ham xuddi shunday ishlaydi. Bir tomondan ma'lumot keladi. Ikkinchi tomondan fikr shakllanadi. Ba'zilar ketadi, ba'zilar qoladi. Xullas...

Shavkatov.io dagi yozuvlar ham shundan paydo bo'lgan. Fikrlar tayyor holatda emas, jarayonda yoziladi. Qanday kelgani va qanday o'zgargani bilan birga.

Bu yerda kirish ham bor, chiqish ham bor.

---

Lekin domen sifatida .io umuman boshqa narsa, rasmiy jihatdan alohida hududga tegishli nom.

P.S. Rasmiy jihatdan .io - Britaniya Hind Okeani hududiga tegishli davlat domeni. AyTi dunyosida u ko'pincha texnik loyihalar va mahsulotlar bilan bog'lanadi.

Norasmiy jihatdan esa unga qanday ma'no berishimizga bog'liq.

<div class="content-en" style="display: none;">

In IT, there is a concept called I/O. Input and Output. In other words, what goes in and what comes out.

The simplest example:

A key pressed on the keyboard - input.

A character appearing on the screen - output.

Technically, that is I/O.

I think life works the same way. Information comes from one side. Thoughts form on the other. Some leave, some stay. Anyway...

The writings on shavkatov.io came from this idea. Thoughts are not written in a finished form, but in the process. Along with how they came and how they changed.

There is input here, and there is output.

---

But as a domain, .io is something completely different, officially a name belonging to a specific territory.

P.S. Officially, .io is a country code domain for the British Indian Ocean Territory. In the IT world, it is often associated with tech projects and products.

Unofficially, it depends on what meaning we give it.
