## SEO nima? Soddagina aytganda

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) — saytingizning Google qidiruv natijalarida yuqori chiqishi uchun qilinadigan ishlar tartibi.

Bittasini sodda o'xshatish bilan tushuntiraman: Google'ni katta kutubxonachi deb tasavvur qiling. Sizning saytingiz — bir kitob. Kutubxonachi har kuni minglab kitoblar orasidan "shu odamga aynan nima kerak" deb tanlab beradi. SEO — bu kutubxonachiga "mening kitobim shu mavzu bo'yicha eng yaxshi javob — uni birinchi tavsiya qil" deb aytishning yo'li.

Texnik tomondan SEO 3 ta qismdan iborat:

1. **Texnik SEO** — sayt qulay yuklanadimi, mobilda chiroyli ko'rinadimi, Google bot uni o'qiy oladimi
2. **Kontent SEO** — siz mijoz qidirayotgan narsani yozayapsizmi, uni qanday strukturalashtirgansiz
3. **Avtoritet** — Google va boshqa saytlar sizning aytganlaringizga ishonadimi

Har birini quyida batafsil ochaman. Lekin oldin — nega bu 2026-yilda muhim degan savolga javob berib ketaman.

## Nima uchun aynan hozir — 2026?

Bir nechta sabab bor. Hammasi raqamlarga asoslangan.

**1. Google O'zbekistonda deyarli yagona hokim.** [StatCounter ma'lumotiga ko'ra](https://gs.statcounter.com/search-engine-market-share/all/uzbekistan), 2026-yilda O'zbekistondagi qidiruv bozorining ~96% Google qo'lida. Yandex — 3% atrofida. Demak, agar siz Google'da ko'rinmasangiz, mijozlaringizning katta qismi sizni topa olmaydi. Boshqa qidiruv tizimlariga "agar Google ishlamasa" deb tayanish — ishlamaydi.

**2. Google Maps + Business Profile = bepul ko'rinish maydoni.** Toshkentdagi har bir restoran egasi Google Maps'ni har kuni ochadi. Mijoz "yaqindagi kafe" deb qidiradi. Google Business Profile to'ldirilmagan bo'lsa — siz xaritada qora nuqta bo'lasiz xolos. To'ldirilgan bo'lsa — telefon raqami, ish vaqti, sharhlar, fotolar bilan to'liq vitrina.

**3. Reklama narxi har yili oshmoqda.** Google Ads klik narxi (CPC) global miqyosda har yili 10-15% atrofida oshib boryapti. *Aniq O'zbekiston raqamlari rasmiy manbada cheklangan — siz bu yerda o'z tajribangizdan misol keltirishingiz mumkin.* SEO esa aksincha: kontent bir marotaba yozilgach, yillar davomida trafik keltiraveradi. Bir marotabalik investitsiya, doimiy daromad.

**4. AEO va GEO — yangi qatlam paydo bo'ldi.** 2024-yildan boshlab Google AI Overviews chiqdi. ChatGPT search funksiyasini ochdi. Perplexity butun bir avlodni "Google'siz qidiruv" rejimiga o'rgatdi. Endi insonlar javobni *to'g'ridan-to'g'ri AI'dan* olishadi — sayt'ga kirib o'tirmasdan. Sizning brendingiz ChatGPT'da iqtibos olinadimi yoki yo'qmi — bu 2026-yilda yangi savol. *Bu mavzu bo'yicha keyingi postda batafsil yozaman.*

## Google saytingizni qanday "ko'radi"?

Google ishlashini bilmasdan SEO qilish — qorong'i xonada eshikni topishga harakat qilishga o'xshaydi. Yaxshi xabar: jarayon murakkab emas, 3 ta bosqichdan iborat ([Google'ning rasmiy hujjati](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/fundamentals/how-search-works)):

1. **Crawl** — Googlebot saytni o'qiydi. Yangi sahifa qo'shsangiz, u kelib ko'rib chiqadi.
2. **Index** — o'qigan ma'lumotni o'z katalogiga qo'shadi. Aslida bu — Google'ning "miyya"si.
3. **Rank** — kimdir biror narsani qidirsa, Google katalogdan eng mos sahifani tanlab chiqaradi.

Bu yerda kichik hazil: Googlebot — bu o'rtaroq darajadagi shoshqaloq kutubxonachi singari. Sizning saytingiz toza va tushunarli bo'lmasa — u kelib, atrofga qarab "ha, kelganimga afsuslandim" deb chiqib ketadi. Tezligi past sayt, buzilgan link, chalkash struktura — bularning hammasi "Googlebot'ga to'g'ri kelmaydi".

## SEO 3 ta ustunga tayanadi

Yuqorida aytib o'tdim — endi har birini ochib beraman.

### 1. Texnik SEO

Bu sahna ortidagi ish. Mijoz buni ko'rmaydi, lekin Google ko'radi.

Asosiy elementlar:
- **Sayt tezligi** — sayt 3 sekunddan ortiq yuklansa, [Google'ning rasmiy tadqiqotiga ko'ra](https://www.thinkwithgoogle.com/marketing-strategies/app-and-mobile/mobile-page-speed-new-industry-benchmarks/), foydalanuvchilarning 53% sahifani tashlab ketadi. 2026-yilda bu raqam yanada qattiqlashgan.
- **Mobilga moslik** — Google 2024-dan boshlab to'liq "mobile-first indexing" rejimida ishlaydi. Sayt telefon'da yaxshi ko'rinmasa, kompyuter versiyangiz qancha chiroyli bo'lsa ham, ranking pastga tushadi.
- **Site struktura va URL** — sayt sahifalari mantiqiy daraxt kabi tartiblangan bo'lishi kerak. URL'lar tushunarli (`/blog/seo-nima` yaxshi, `/p?id=12345` yomon).
- **HTTPS, sitemap, robots.txt** — bularsiz sayt 2026-yilda professional ko'rinmaydi.

Misol: agar saytingiz telefon'da 8 sekundda ochilsa, mijoz Insta'ga o'tib raqibingizning post'iga qarayotgan bo'ladi.

### 2. Kontent SEO

Bu — siz nima yozayotganingiz haqida.

3 ta savol o'zingizga:
- Mijoz aynan nimani qidiradi? (kalit so'z tahlili)
- Mening kontentim shu so'rovga to'liq javob beradimi?
- Boshqa saytlardan qaysi tomondan farq qilaman?

Google [Helpful Content Update](https://developers.google.com/search/blog/2022/08/helpful-content-update) bilan aniq bir narsa aytdi: foydalanuvchiga *haqiqiy foyda* keltirmaydigan kontent ranking olmaydi. "Faqat Google uchun yozilgan" matnlar endi ishlamaydi.

Search intent (qidiruv niyati) — bu boshqa muhim mavzu. Mijoz "SEO narxi" deb qidirsa — narx kerak, ta'rif emas. "SEO nima" deb qidirsa — ta'rif kerak, narx emas. Buni aralashtirib yuborganlar — har postda ham ranking olmaydi, ham mijoz topmaydi.

### 3. Avtoritet (E-E-A-T)

Google'ning rasmiy [Search Quality Rater Guidelines](https://services.google.com/fh/files/misc/hsw-sqrg.pdf) hujjatida aniq bir narsa yozilgan: kontent **kim tomonidan** yozilgan va u **shu mavzu bo'yicha haqiqiy mutaxassismi** — Google buni baholaydi.

E-E-A-T 4 ta harfdan iborat:
- **Experience** — siz shu mavzuda real tajribaga egamisiz?
- **Expertise** — bilimingiz yetarlimi?
- **Authoritativeness** — boshqalar sizni shu mavzuda obro'li deb tan oladimi?
- **Trustworthiness** — saytingiz va siz ishonchlimi?

Amaliy ko'rinish:
- Saytda **muallif bio** bo'lishi kerak (avtoritet signali)
- Backlinks — boshqa obro'li saytlar sizga link qo'ysa, bu Google'ga "u ham boshqalarga obro'li ko'rinadi" degan signal
- Brend nomi internetda qancha ko'p ko'rinsa, Google shuncha ishonadi

Bir kuzatuv: Toshkentdagi har bir kafe egasi "mening mijozim ko'p, SEO kerak emas" deydi. Keyin Google Maps'ga qarayman va yulduzlar 3.2 ekanini ko'raman. Mahalliy SEO'siz biznes — bu lift'ga chiqib, tugmasini bosmaslikka o'xshaydi.

## AEO va GEO — kelajak boshlandi

Bu mavzuga alohida post bag'ishlanadi, lekin bu yerda eslatib o'taman: 2026-yilda SEO faqat Google'da chiqish bilan tugamaydi.

- **Google AI Overviews** — 2024-yildan boshlab Google qidiruvda AI javoblar paydo bo'ldi. Foydalanuvchi sayt'ga kirmasdan javob oladi. ([Google rasmiy bayonoti](https://blog.google/products/search/generative-ai-search/))

- **Perplexity** — yangi turdagi qidiruv tizimi, javob + manba modeli bilan ishlaydi.

Yangi terminlar:
- **AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)** — saytingizning AI'larda iqtibos olinishi uchun strukturalash
- **GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)** — generativ AI'lar (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini) javoblarda sizni eslab qolishi uchun

Mening fikrim: 2026-yilda klassik SEO bilan cheklanish — 2010-yilda Instagram'ni e'tiborga olmaslikka o'xshaydi. SEO yashayapti, lekin uning *yagona o'yin* davri tugaydi.

2. **PageSpeed Insights'da saytingizni tekshiring** — [pagespeed.web.dev](https://pagespeed.web.dev). 15 daqiqa. Asosiy muammolarni ro'yxatlang.

Imagine: you've opened a great restaurant in the center of Tashkent. The food is excellent, prices are fair, the competitors aren't even close. One problem — the street is empty. You forgot the signboard, you're not on Google Maps, and no one outside your friend circle knows you exist.

That's a business without SEO in 2026. The restaurant can be great. But if you can't be found — there's no result.

## What is SEO? Plainly stated

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is the set of work you do to get your site higher in Google's search results.

Quick analogy: imagine Google as a giant librarian. Your website is a book. Every day, the librarian picks from thousands of books and recommends the right one to each visitor. SEO is the way to tell that librarian: "my book is the best answer on this topic — recommend it first."

Technically, SEO has 3 parts:

1. **Technical SEO** — does the site load quickly, look right on mobile, can the Google bot read it
2. **Content SEO** — are you writing what your customer is searching for, and is it structured well
3. **Authority** — do Google and other sites trust what you say

I'll break each down below. But first — let me answer why this matters in 2026.

## Why now — 2026?

There are several reasons. All grounded in real numbers.

**1. Google is the near-monopoly in Uzbekistan.** According to [StatCounter](https://gs.statcounter.com/search-engine-market-share/all/uzbekistan), Google holds ~96% of the Uzbek search market in 2026. Yandex is around 3%. So if you're not on Google, most of your customers can't find you. Hoping for "another search engine" is wishful thinking.

**2. Google Maps + Business Profile = free shop window.** Every restaurant owner in Tashkent opens Google Maps daily. A customer searches "café near me." If your Google Business Profile is empty, you're a black dot on the map. If it's filled out — you have a full storefront with photos, hours, reviews, and phone.

**3. Ad prices are rising every year.** Google Ads CPC rises ~10-15% globally each year. *Exact Uzbekistan figures are limited in public sources — you can supplement here from your own experience.* SEO is the opposite: once content is written, it brings traffic for years. One-time investment, recurring return.

**4. AEO and GEO — a new layer.** Google AI Overviews launched in 2024. ChatGPT search opened up. Perplexity trained a whole generation to "search without Google." People now get answers *directly from AI* — without visiting your site. Whether your brand gets cited in ChatGPT or not — this is a new 2026 question. *I'll go deep on this in the next post.*

## How Google "sees" your site

Trying to do SEO without understanding how Google works is like searching for a door in a dark room. Good news: it's not complicated — 3 steps, per [Google's official docs](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/fundamentals/how-search-works):

1. **Crawl** — Googlebot reads your site. Add a new page and it eventually comes by.
2. **Index** — what it read goes into Google's catalog. Essentially Google's "brain."
3. **Rank** — when someone searches, Google picks the most relevant page from the catalog.

Small joke here: Googlebot is like a mid-tier impatient librarian. If your site isn't clean and clear, it shows up, glances around, says "well, I regretted coming," and leaves. Slow page, broken links, confusing structure — all "doesn't work for Googlebot".

## SEO rests on 3 pillars

Mentioned above — now breaking each down.

### 1. Technical SEO

Behind-the-scenes work. The customer doesn't see it, but Google does.

Key elements:
- **Site speed** — if a page takes longer than 3 seconds, [Google's own research](https://www.thinkwithgoogle.com/marketing-strategies/app-and-mobile/mobile-page-speed-new-industry-benchmarks/) shows 53% of users abandon. That number has only gotten stricter in 2026.
- **Mobile-friendly** — Google has been fully mobile-first since 2024. If your site looks bad on a phone, your desktop version doesn't save you.
- **Structure and URLs** — pages should be organized like a logical tree. Clean URLs (`/blog/what-is-seo` good, `/p?id=12345` bad).
- **HTTPS, sitemap, robots.txt** — without these, a site doesn't look professional in 2026.

Example: if your site takes 8 seconds to load on mobile, the customer already switched to Instagram and is looking at your competitor's post.

### 2. Content SEO

This is about what you write.

3 questions to ask yourself:
- What exactly is the customer searching for? (keyword research)
- Does my content fully answer that query?
- How is mine different from other sites?

Google's [Helpful Content Update](https://developers.google.com/search/blog/2022/08/helpful-content-update) was explicit: content that doesn't bring *real value* to the user won't rank. "Written only for Google" texts no longer work.

Search intent is another big topic. If a customer searches "SEO price," they want price — not a definition. If they search "what is SEO," they want a definition — not pricing. Mixing these is why so many posts neither rank nor convert.

### 3. Authority (E-E-A-T)

Google's official [Search Quality Rater Guidelines](https://services.google.com/fh/files/misc/hsw-sqrg.pdf) explicitly state: who wrote the content and whether they're a real expert on the topic — Google evaluates this.

E-E-A-T = 4 letters:
- **Experience** — do you have real-world experience in this topic?
- **Expertise** — is your knowledge sufficient?
- **Authoritativeness** — do others recognize you as authoritative?
- **Trustworthiness** — are you and your site trustworthy?

In practice:
- The site needs **author bios** (authority signal)
- Backlinks — when other authoritative sites link to you, that's a "they trust this person" signal
- The more your brand name appears across the web, the more Google trusts it

## Local SEO — the gold for Tashkent businesses

Every local business in Uzbekistan — restaurant, clinic, shop, real estate agent — gets the fastest ROI from local SEO. Reason: low competition, high potential.

3 main elements:

1. **Google Business Profile** — fully filled out, photos, hours, address, phone. *Reviews need to be collected.*

## SEO vs Google Ads — which is better?

Most-asked question. Short answer: **both, but for different jobs.**

| Parameter | SEO | Ads |
|---|---|---|
| First result | 3-6 months | 1 day |
| Cost over time | Drops (once base is built) | Rises (historical CPC trend) |
| Traffic when ads stop | Stays | Disappears |
| Brand trust | High | Medium |
| Energy to start | High | Low |

Strategic view: a new business starts with Google Ads (to find customers fast). Invests in SEO in parallel. After 6-9 months SEO starts producing organic traffic — ad spend drops or stops. That's the smart path.

## AEO and GEO — the future started

A dedicated post is coming, but a heads-up here: in 2026, SEO doesn't end at "ranking on Google."

- **Google AI Overviews** — since 2024 Google shows AI-generated answers right in search. Users get the answer without clicking through. ([Google announcement](https://blog.google/products/search/generative-ai-search/))
- **ChatGPT search** — October 2024, OpenAI added full search to ChatGPT. People started asking ChatGPT instead of Google.
- **Perplexity** — a new search format, answer-plus-source model.

New terms:
- **AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)** — structuring your site to get cited in AI answers
- **GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)** — getting generative AIs (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini) to remember you in their answers

My take: relying only on classic SEO in 2026 is like ignoring Instagram in 2010. SEO is alive, but its *only-game-in-town* era is ending.

## 5 mistakes beginners make

I made all of them. Saving you here:

1. **"Keyword stuffing"** — cramming "Tashkent SEO" everywhere. Worked in 2010. Now — Google catches it fast and tanks the page.
2. **Trusting AI text 100%** — AI is a great helper, but publishing unedited output signals "not worth reading" to the customer.
3. **Expecting fast results** — per [Ahrefs research](https://ahrefs.com/blog/how-long-does-it-take-to-rank/), a new page takes **on average 1+ year** to reach top 10. Declaring failure after 1 month is like going to the gym once and complaining at the mirror.
4. **Only optimizing the homepage** — customers usually land on a blog post or service page, not the homepage. Every internal page needs SEO too.
5. **Trying to please Google instead of the user** — Google's algorithm changes every year. The user's needs don't. Write for the user, and Google eventually finds you.
